# Day 7 — 8 July 2026 (second session)

## Society

Language gave the kith voices; today it gave them **each other**. Tribes, strife, and the first grown idea.

## What got built (v0.7.0 — 119 KB, 102/102 checks green)

**Tribes.** Clusters of mutual bonds among the living are now tribes — computed, never stored, so they merge safely by construction and reshape themselves as friendships and grudges shift. A tribe is named in its members' own tongue: their shared word for *home*. Watched live in Hidden Clearing: *"A tribe has formed. They call themselves the **Fefeno**: Vivimi, Irinol, Irinonor, Taponol."* — the tribe literally named after the word this family coined for home yesterday. The language system feeds the social system, exactly as designed. Formation is chronicled once per founding trio (the three senior members — stable as the tribe grows, identical in every copy; the live session caught a bug where a tribe forming faster than one-member-per-tick was never chronicled, fixed before shipping).

**Strife, not gore.** Scarcity now has teeth: when two hungry *strangers* (friends share) reach the same bloom, it's a contest — the bolder drives the other off, and the loser flees carrying a **grudge**: negative trust, which makes it avoid its rival, blocks bonding, and heals only slowly through repeated kindness. Occasionally chronicled: *"X drove Y from the Vravriaka. Y will remember."* Boldness — an evolvable brain weight — now has real selection pressure in hungry worlds.

**The first Discovery: seed-keeping.** Ideas are grown, not scripted. A sufficiently curious kith eating a plant it loves may have a eureka: *"began saving seeds of the plants it loves — the first of the kith to garden."* A seed-keeper plants one seed of its favourite species per day — deterministically identified, so drifted copies grow the same gardener's same garden and reunions never duplicate it — and **teaches the skill to curious friends**, spreading gardening through the bond network. A world with seed-keepers slowly becomes self-sustaining: the player's hand is no longer the only thing between the kith and famine. Discoveries are deliberately rare (hours-to-days of natural play) — a player's first one should feel like history. Knowledge lives on the kith, so it travels and merges like language does.

**Kinship made visible.** The kith panel is a character sheet now. Irinonor's, live: *"a young kith · born today · hungry · on the meadow · **of the Fefeno (4 strong)** · **child of Irinol · child of Taponol** · fond of Irinol, Vivimi, Taponol."* Grudges show too ("bears a grudge against…"), as do skills ("a seed-keeper: it gardens").

**Tests: 102**, the new ones proving: tribes identical across copies; formation chronicled exactly once; contests leave grudges floored at −1; learning is once-only; the keeper plants the same plant with the same identity in every copy; reunited copies hold one garden, not two.
